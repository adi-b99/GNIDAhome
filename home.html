Welcome To Greater Noida Authority

Greater Noida Industrial Area is located at the intersection of the Western and Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridors and is also the gateway to the Delhi-Mumbai Industrial Corridor (DMIC). Greater Noida is being developed as Metro centre providing for quality urban environment, to attract economic activities and population to decongest Delhi.
